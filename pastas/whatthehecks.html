Farfalle
" far-fal-leh "

What: "Butterflies" Farfalle is a bow tie shaped pasta. The outer edges of this pasta are smooth and straight, or crinkled with a scallop-type edge, and the center is pinched or crimped together in order to form the bow-tie shape.
